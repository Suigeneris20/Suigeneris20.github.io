For a Woman Worth Everything

Twas an arid night like any other
The fallen dew fouled the air like cannon fodder
When solemn winds stirred passive thoughts
And menacing gusts made me shudder
There was a cozy warmth I was soon to know
An enchanting gaze I could only follow. . .
With foggy mist still on my brow
And as the personification of the ailing word, “wow!"
I asked a homely schelling:
“what gets you going:”

Soon I walked an exhilarating path
Amidst the fortunes of cause and wrath
As the clouds thundered glum
I knew a light fuzzy and warm
As the crescent moon its final glow
Came a mirth-y smile in my heart sown

I am lucky this path I walked
For while the road occasionally forked
In its essence I found reason and rhyme
As the soulless bask in infinite time
We found grace in fleeting memory
And since forever is not my luxury
I basked in my found glee
And a charm that’s equal parts
Lovely and equal parts goofball

Herein lies my “thank you”
For the countless laughs
Thank you
For your company and half
Through existential angst
Thank you
For coloring my seams
Thank you
For your nuanced whim
Thank you

In all I am grateful for getting to know you, for the memories we made, that I’ll hold dear, for the night at the gym, for your odyssey from India, for the day you were born. For that day serves as the harbinger of this indelible experience. SO, here’s to the day the universe was graced by your birth: Happy birthday!! I will you many many more of these celebrations.

Now I can invoke full circle

HAPPY BIRTHDAY Sheetal!
